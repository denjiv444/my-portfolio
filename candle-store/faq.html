How should I burn my candle for best results?

For optimal performance and longevity:

  • First burn: Allow wax to melt across entire surface (3-4 hours)
  • Wick care: Trim to 1/4" before each lighting
  • Burn time: Never exceed 4 hours per session
  • Between uses: Let wax solidify before relighting
  • Final use: Stop when 1/2" of wax remains

Artisan's Tip: Our soy wax burns cleaner and longer than paraffin, with no toxic emissions.

Are your candles safe for pets and children?

While our natural candles are safer than conventional options, we recommend:

  • Keep out of reach of children and pets
  • Burn in well-ventilated areas
  • Consider our unscented options for sensitive individuals
  • Never leave burning candles unattended
  • Extinguish if any signs of discomfort are observed

Note: Birds are particularly sensitive to essential oils - avoid burning scented candles around them.

How can I fix tunneling or uneven burning?

To correct or prevent tunneling:

  • Always allow full melt pool on first burn
  • Use a candle warmer to even out wax
  • Wrap aluminum foil around the rim to concentrate heat
  • Rotate candle occasionally if near drafts
